This Privacy Policy describes how Market Trends Explorer ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our application. This policy is provided to comply with the Login with Amazon (LWA) requirements.

When you use our application to log in via Amazon, we may request access to certain information from your Amazon account. This is strictly limited to the information necessary to provide our research and analytics services.

  • Login Information: Your name, email address, and Amazon profile information as authorized by you during the Login with Amazon process.

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information and Amazon data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
